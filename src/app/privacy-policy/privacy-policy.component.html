Welcome to Join! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Join, and your rights concerning your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Join, we may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Personal Information: This includes information you provide to us directly, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User account details (e.g., username)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information: This includes data collected when you interact with Join, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device information (e.g., operating system)
  • Usage data, such as actions performed

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To personalize your experience on Join
  • To improve our platform's functionality and performance
  • To communicate with you, including updates, newsletters, and support
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following scenarios:

  • With service providers to assist in delivering the services of Join
  • In response to legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders
  • To protect the rights and safety of Join, its users, or others
4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: The right to access the personal data we have collected about you
  • Correction: The right to request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion: The right to request the deletion of your personal data
  • Data Portability: The right to request a copy of your personal data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: The right to withdraw your consent at any time
5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with a new "Effective Date."

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@paul-ivan.com.
